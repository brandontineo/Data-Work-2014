PHOTO RESTORATION

Flashphoto Photography has been committed to offering the Bay Area the best quality of photos, and photo restoration is no exception. With 20 years of experience, Flashphoto works with highly professional equipment to renew your old photos. Bring ripped, torn, faded, dirtied, water effected, or even blurry photos and we will fix and renew them. With our new equipment it is now possible to resotre your old photos without risking damage to your originals. We work not only to repair notable damages to your images but also fix color, contrast, sharpness, saturation and every little detail that can make your photo look more stunning! PHOTO ALTERATION

Perhaps you took a photo and you love the pose, the way you look, the clothes you're wearing and the people your with, but the background isn't your favorite. Our photo alteration service deal with these types of photos on a daily basis. We can change the background of a photo, fix the landing of a wedding dress and even elimante unwanted people from images in a way that makes the photo look natural. If a photo didn't come out the way you wanted, you can make it the way you want it!
