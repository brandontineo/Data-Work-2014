PASSPORT PHOTOS

You can always count on Flashphoto for high quality and fast passport photos. It doesn't matter what color background you need, or what size you need. Our professional passport system enable to take passport photos with white, blue, gray, cream, light blue, yellow and even green background! Remember that every country has different regulations when it comes to passport photos. Flashphoto has all of these requirements on file for your convenience. No hassles!


    Our Passports are the best in the Bay Area for a number of reasons:

  • They only take 5 minutees. No long waits!
  • We use a digital system that enables us to take several shots until you look perfect!
  • We offer only the highest quality and that means no blurs, no shadows, no closed eyes, and no mistakes!
  • We can show you the image before is gets prints
    •       You must inform the photographer you wish to see the image before it gets printed.
  • Our professional photographers have 10-20 years of experience and training which ensures they can take excellent quality photos.

"I'm not photogenic!" Thats no problem for us. Many of our customers are suprised at how great they look in pictures after taking passport photos with us!

AT HOME PASSPORT PHOTOS OR DISABLED

If you have a relative or friend who needs to take their passport photos at home, we can do that too. People in wheelchairs, sick in bed, or simply unable to physically visit our studio may request our "we come to you service". Our portable professional equipment allows us to visit customers at home and take their photos there. No hassles and no delays! Taking your passport photos at home are printed in the same quality as those taken at our studio.
