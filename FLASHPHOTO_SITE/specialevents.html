Photographers: We have several photographers available to go out to your special event. Whether it be in South San Francisco or the rest of the Bay Area, Flashphoto ensures you that the photographer will be there on time and for the time needed. Our photographers have over 20 years of Experience photographing Special Events and that means high quality photos on your special day.

* Our packages for Photos for Special Events are comfortable and flexible. We are ready to meet your budget and needs. * Most Special Events Include: Mass, Outdoor Photoshoot, and Reception. .

* You can pay your packages in parts. Payments can be made weekly, bi-weekly, or even monthly. It's your special event, so its your choice how you want to pay for it!


*Videographers: The Videographers have over 20 years of experience recording Weddings, Quinceaneros, Sweet Sixteens, Renewing of the Vows, and Baptisms.

* Typically, the videographer captures the most important moments of the special event. This includes mass and reception. Most receptions are scheduled in a way that includes special dances, and toasts.

*The Videographer, upon request, can even interview various family members, relatives, and guests. Many times the Photographer will give away free wallet size photos (please ask for details).

* The Special Event Video includes: an Introduction consisting of Music, Text, Special Effects, Photos etc... the Mass, and the Reception + Credits.

Reserving Your Photographer/Videographer

It is recommended that you Reserve a photographer for your Special Event in advance in order to make sure that there will be someone available on your special day. Reserving your photographer in advance also means that you can pay in parts, weekly, bi-weekly, and even monthly. This convenience works perfectly for couples who are a bit tight on budget.

To fill out your contract and reserve your Photographer you musts schedule an appointment to speak with one of the Photographers. He/She can show you the various packages we have and even some examples of Videos and Photos of other customers. Give us a call at (650) 737-6044 and schedule your appointment today!
